Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

  • One 9-inch pie
  • 30 min-prep times
  • 10 min-cook times
  • 40 min-total
  • 8 servings

INGRDENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flours
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 lemon, juice and tested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 eggs yolks, beaten
  • 1(9 9-inch)pie crust,backed
  • 4 eggs white
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTION

  1. Perheat Over:Perheat over to 35o degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling:in the medium saucepan...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour,cornstarch, and salt
    • Stir in water,Lemon,Juice and lemon zest
    • cook over medium-height heat stiving frequently,until mixture come to boil
    • stir in butter
    • place eggs yolks in small bowel and gradually whisk in 1/2 cups of hot water
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constatly until thick
    • Remove from heat
    • pour filling baked pastry shell
  3. Make Meringue :in the large glass or metal bowl
    • whip eggs whits util foamy
    • Add sugar gradually and continue to whip until stiff peaks form
    • Spead mergigue over pie ,sealing the egges the edges at the crust.
  4. Bake:Bake in the spread oven for 10 minutes or until the mergigue golden brown
